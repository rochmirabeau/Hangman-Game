3rd Strike Hangman

Guess The character's name before you strike out!

Your mission is to guess the name of the Street Fighter 3: Third Strike character.
You will guess letters by typing.
If the letter you guess is in the character's name, it will be revealed, if not, you will get a strike.
Failure to guess the character's name before your strike count reaches 7 will result in a game over
Press the Start button to begin
